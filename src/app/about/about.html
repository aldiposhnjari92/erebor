Our story

We built SubTrack because
SaaS billing is a mess

It started with a spreadsheet, an exit interview, and $800/month in subscriptions nobody remembered signing up for.

The problem nobody was solving

In 2022, our co-founders were running a 12-person product team. When a key engineer left, they did an offboarding audit and found 11 active SaaS subscriptions tied to his company email — tools the team hadn't used in months.

The exercise revealed a pattern: duplicate tools bought by different departments, annual plans forgotten after the first month, free trials that auto-converted years ago. The team was paying over $800/month for subscriptions they didn't need.

They looked for a tool to fix this. Nothing good existed. So they built Erebor.
